Market Highlights

Fresh Seafood Selection

Fresh Seafood Paradise

The heart of Nong Mon Market features an incredible selection of fresh and dried seafood. From sun-dried squid and shrimp to razor clams and fish sauce, this section offers the finest coastal delicacies that local fishermen bring in daily.

Traditional Thai Desserts

Traditional Thai Desserts

Nong Mon Market is famous for its authentic Thai desserts, especially Kanom Chak (grilled sweet sticky rice flour in nipa palm leaves) and Khao Lam (sweet custardy sticky rice cooked in bamboo tubes). These treats represent the region's culinary heritage.

Desserts Traditional Heritage

Central Market Area

Ready-to-Eat Delicacies

Prepared Food Specialties

Experience freshly prepared local dishes including deep-fried fish, Hor Mok (Thai curry fish custard), and Hoi Jo Pu (deep-fried crab meat roll). These authentic flavors showcase the best of coastal Thai cuisine prepared by skilled local vendors.

Monkey Hill Viewpoint

A hilltop viewpoint home to hundreds of monkeys and offering spectacular views of the coastline. The shrine at the top is believed to bring good luck to visitors.

Market Hours & Best Times

Early Morning

6:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Best time for fresh seafood and traditional desserts. Cooler temperatures and full vendor selection.

Mid-Day

10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Peak activity hours with all vendors open. Great for prepared foods and shopping for souvenirs.

Evening

4:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Cooler temperatures return. Perfect for leisurely browsing and enjoying prepared foods before closing.

Nong Mon Market operates daily from 6:00 AM to 7:00 PM, with the best selection available in the morning when fresh products arrive.

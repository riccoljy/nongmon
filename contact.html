Getting to Nong Mon Market

By Car

Drive from Bangkok via Highway 7 (Motorway) to Chonburi, then take Sukhumvit Road to Nong Mon Market.

Distance: ~64 miles from Bangkok

Duration: 1.5-2 hours

By Bus

Take a bus from Bangkok's Eastern Bus Terminal (Ekkamai) to Chonburi, then local transport to Nong Mon Market on Sukhumvit Road.

Frequency: Every 30 minutes

Cost: ~100-150 THB

By Train

Take the train from Bangkok to Chonburi Railway Station, then take a songthaew to Nong Mon Market.

Duration: 2-3 hours total

Budget-friendly option

Market Hours & Information

Operating Hours

Open daily from 6:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Best selection available in the morning hours.

Daily: 6:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Location

Located on Sukhumvit Road near Bang Saen Beach, easily accessible by car or public transport.

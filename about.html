Our Mission

To showcase the authentic culinary heritage and cultural richness of Nong Mon Market through the unique perspective of international and local students, fostering cross-cultural understanding and promoting appreciation for traditional Thai market culture.

This project represents more than just market promotion — it's a bridge between Singapore and Thailand, connecting young minds through shared culinary experiences and creating lasting relationships that transcend borders.
